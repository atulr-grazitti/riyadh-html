RIYADH ART
VISION

To contribute to strengthening cultural and artistic aspects across the city by implementing 1000 artworks by international and local artists.

In turn, this will contribute in improving quality of life in Riyadh, and emphasize the city’s position as an International destination combining modernity and authenticity to meet the Kingdom’s Vision 2030
